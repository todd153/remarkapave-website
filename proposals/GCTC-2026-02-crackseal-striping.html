Scope Notes

  • Unit price basis: Crack seal priced per LF; striping priced per plan-set counts. Final billing follows field-verified quantities at the rates shown.
  • Crack seal materials/prep: Cracks 1/8"–3/4" wide, cleaned by wire brush and compressed air, sealed with hot-applied ASTM D6690 rubberized sealant squeegeed flush.
  • Striping materials: Commercial-grade acrylic traffic paint with machine-applied retroreflective glass beads on all markings (included). White stalls, blue ADA fields, yellow pole bases; one machine color change included.
  • Mobilization: Crack seal and striping each include 2 mobilizations at $250 ($500 per scope). Lots must be clear at scheduled arrival ($250 return mobilization if blocked, excluding weather); added or split-out mobilizations $250 each.
  • Schedule / hours: Work performed after normal business hours and/or weekends to keep the campus in use. Striping performed after crack seal (and seal coat under separate proposal WE-2026-02) has cured.
  • Weather: Requires clean, dry pavement, surface temperature 50°F and rising. No application on wet, frozen, or contaminated surfaces.
  • Optional services: Heat-lance cleanout and rout-and-seal are available at the rates shown and added by selection above or written change order.
  • Excludes: Seal coat (separate proposal WE-2026-02), pavement/base repairs, patching of alligatored or failed areas (change order), permits/traffic control unless stated.
Cracks wider than 3/4": Cracks exceeding 3/4" in width, alligatored/interconnected cracking, and base failure are excluded from the pricing above. Hot-pour sealant applied outside its rated width fails prematurely and can create surface hazards. Such areas will be documented and quoted separately as mastic repair or patching.
