Scope Notes

  • Unit price basis: Crack sealing priced on an estimated 10,000 LF. Final billing follows field-measured linear footage at the rate shown.
  • Crack width: Pricing covers cracks 1/8" to 3/4" in width, cleaned by wire brush and compressed air prior to sealant application.
  • After-hours / weekend work: Work performed after normal business hours and/or on weekends to maintain facility traffic flow; schedule window coordinated with the Contractor.
  • Weather: Requires clean, dry pavement with surface temperature 50°F and rising. No application on wet, frozen, or contaminated surfaces.
  • Mobilization: One mobilization included. Lots must be clear and accessible at scheduled arrival ($200 return mobilization if blocked); additional mobilizations $200 each.
  • Optional services: Heat-lance cleanout and rout-and-seal are available at the rates shown and added by selection above or written change order.
  • Excludes: Line striping (quoted separately under companion proposal), seal coat (separate proposal), traffic control/permits unless stated, and pavement repairs beyond crack sealing.
Cracks wider than 3/4": Cracks exceeding 3/4" in width, alligatored/interconnected cracking, and areas of base failure are excluded from the pricing above. Hot-pour sealant applied outside its rated width fails prematurely and can create surface hazards. Such areas will be documented and quoted separately as mastic repair or patching upon request.
