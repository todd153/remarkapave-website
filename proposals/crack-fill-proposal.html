Crack Sealing Proposal

Attn: Craig Glover · ctglover@gloverai.com General Contractor: Central Contractors, Inc. · Robin Perkins · (918) 351-1404 Project: Ponca Tribe — Crack Seal, Ponca City, OK Date: July 13, 2026 Proposal #: RP-CF-0001 Valid for: 30 days Terms: Net 30

Scope of Work

RemarkaPave LLC will furnish all labor, materials, and equipment to clean and hot-pour crack seal approximately 10,000 linear feet of asphalt cracks at the above location. Work includes:

  • Mechanical cleaning of cracks by wire brush and high-pressure air to remove dirt, vegetation, and loose debris
  • Application of hot-applied, polymer-modified rubberized crack sealant (ASTM D6690) to cracks 1/8" to 3/4" in width
  • Sealant squeegeed flush to the pavement surface for a clean, durable band
  • Cleanup and haul-off of debris generated by the work
