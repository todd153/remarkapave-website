WHITE EAGLE CAMPUS — CRACK SEAL & LINE STRIPING
Client: Central Contractors, Inc. — Attn: Craig Glover Site: Ponca Tribe White Eagle Campus · 101 White Eagle Dr, Ponca City, OK 74601 Plans: Brown Engineering plan set (STRIPPING01, Project PROJ1504) Base scope: Crack seal 10,000 LF + full striping per plans
Quantities below derive from the accepted GCTC-2026-02 scope and standard coverage rates. Crack sealant is a solid calculation. Estimate — confirm flags paint & beads: painted-area math is approximate until the field layout and paint colors are confirmed in writing — verify counts before buying.

Before you buy paint or beads: the gallon figures are area-based estimates, not field-measured. Confirm the final stall/aisle counts and get paint colors in writing (white / blue / fire-lane yellow-or-red) before ordering. Order single colors needing 3+ gal in 5-gal pails. Crack-sealant quantity is solid and safe to buy now.
